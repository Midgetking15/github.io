Experimental design

Fibonacci Generative Designs

Born out of the Fibonacci Sequence, this design was created in grasshopper by having plane spawn points procedurally generate out of center based on the sequence. Planes were then given dimensions based on closest points and specialized parameters.

Topology Optimized Pen

Created using NX frustum this functional pen was created to have a low volume while still being able to withstand normal forces. It was given forces along normal contact points of a pen and then given specialized parameters based on volume and those forces. It was then touched up in MeshMixer and printed on an SLA machine.
